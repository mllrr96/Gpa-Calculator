> üéì **Note:** This app is specifically designed for students of [The American University of Iraq, Sulaimani (AUIS)](https://auis.edu.krd/), and follows the official GPA calculation policy described in [How to Calculate Your GPA](https://auis.edu.krd/how-calculate-your-gpa).

- Light/Dark mode support

## üéì UG vs MBA GPA Rules

- ‚úÖ **UG**: Allows grades from A to F (including C-, D+, D)
